Lesson 7.4: Authentic Voice in Your Writing
Chapter 7: AI in Media | Project Day | Creative Writing and Voice Development
Lesson Overview: Students experience both sides of AI creation—what it can do and what it can't—then turn the lens on their own creative work. They discover that AI works best as a mirror: bring yourself, and AI can help you create; ask AI to generate meaning from nothing, and you get emptiness. Through a flat paragraph transformation activity, students experience how their specific choices bring writing to life. They then analyze their own creative projects to identify passages with authentic voice versus passages that could be stronger. The lesson introduces "Beyond the 70% Rule," showing how expert-level AI collaboration involves front-loaded contribution (character, plot, themes) rather than just word count.
